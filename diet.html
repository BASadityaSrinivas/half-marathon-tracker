🍱 7-Day Diet Chart

Each day is fully decided — no choosing. Tap a day, eat exactly that.
The deal: eat the portions shown, no random extras, 1 sweet/day. That's the gentle deficit (~0.3–0.5 kg/wk) — we don't starve, the food fuels your training. Portions are the instruction; kcal notes are just for awareness.

✋ How to measure — no scale needed

You overeat by habit, not hunger — so control volume with your hand + one fixed bowl. Precision isn't the point; consistency is.
  • Carbs (rice/dosa) = 1 fist / 1 level katori
  • Protein (eggs/dal/paneer) = 1 palm
  • Veg = 2 fists (eat freely)
  • Snacks (chana/nuts) = 1 cupped hand
  • Dry fruits/fats = 1 thumb

🍽️ Beat overeating (matters more than any number)

  • Serve once, put the pot away — plate it in the kitchen, then sit. No refills in reach. (Biggest lever.)
  • Smaller plate — your brain reads a full plate as a full meal.
  • Water before every meal + eat slow, wait 15 min before seconds (fullness lags 20 min).
  • Never eat straight from the container.

🎉 Free meal (not "cheat")

One planned free meal a week — eat whatever you want, zero guilt, then straight back to the chart next meal. It's a tool against bingeing, not a slip. Slot: Saturday lunch (earned after your long run). Plus your 1 sweet/day means nothing's ever forbidden.
